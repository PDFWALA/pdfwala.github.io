About PDFWala

PDFWala is a free, browser-based PDF toolkit built for everyone who needs to work with PDF files quickly — without downloading software, creating accounts, or paying for subscriptions.

We believe that basic PDF tools should be free, fast, and private. That's why every tool on PDFWala runs entirely in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. What you do with your documents stays on your device.

Whether you're a student merging assignment files, a professional protecting confidential documents, or someone simply converting a photo to PDF — PDFWala is built for you.
